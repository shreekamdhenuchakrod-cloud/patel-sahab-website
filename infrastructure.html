Mechanized Excellence

Our High-End Sourcing & Processing Machinery

👁️

1. Sortex Seed Cleaners

To guarantee zero physical debris, dust, or organic farm weeds, raw whole cumin, coriander and mustard seeds undergo rigorous multi-step Sortex optical clearing. Damaged or discolored particles are eliminated automatically.

❄️

2. Cryogenic Low-Temp Milling

Instead of traditional warm iron grinding, we employ custom low-speed cool pulverizing chambers. Mill friction levels stay strictly below 30°C to secure volatile flavor oils, color saturation, and inherent medicinal metrics.

🧼

3. Sanitized Pack Enclosures

Our ground spice powders are directly funneled into automatic FFS (Form Fill Seal) packaging setups inside negative-pressure cleanroom areas. Human contact is completely minimized to block microbial contamination risks.
