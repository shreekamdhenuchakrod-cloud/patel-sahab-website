⚙️ General & Grinding Technology

Standard high-speed industrial grinding mills generate high frictional temperatures (often reaching 60°C to 70°C). This extreme heat evaporates the volatile essential oils (like curcumin in turmeric or linalool in coriander) responsible for color and aroma.

At Patel Sahab Spices, we utilize custom-engineered low-speed Cold Grinding Technology. Grinding chambers remain under 30°C. This safely seals 100% of the active medicinal volatile elements and aroma locked inside the packaging layers.

Absolutely. We operate under a strict 100% Clean Label Commitment. Typical commercial brands add dynamic starches (such as boiled potato or wheat flour) to inflate volume, alongside toxic color enhancers (like Sudan Red or Lead Chromate) to hide dull colors.

Our spices undergo rigorous lab testing verifying absolute zero added starch, zero chemical preservatives, and zero color dyes. What you see and smell is 100% ground nature.

🔬 Logistics, Quality Controls & Testing

Milling machinery parts wear down over time, creating a risk of fine iron dust contamination. Every gram of ground spice at Patel Sahab Spices passes through high-density rare-earth magnets and advanced electronic metal-detector coils to automatically isolate and discard any heavy-metal traces.

Our shipping timeline depends directly on the volume parameters and custom milling specs requested. Typically, full container load cargos (FCL) are processed, sanitized, packed, and loaded at ports within 15 to 21 business days from initial deposit clearance.
